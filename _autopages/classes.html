Mini Courses

These courses are offered periodically. Available sessions are listed below.

A $50 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your spot in any class starting more than 2 weeks before the date you sign up, and is applied to the cost of the class. The remainder of the class fee must be paid in full within two weeks of the class start date. Payment may be cash, check, Visa, or MasterCard.

If the class you are attending starts within 2 weeks of the date you are signing up, we ask that you pay for the class in full at the time you register.
